Overview

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Wake Me ("the App") is a location-based alarm application developed by A²H Software. This Privacy Policy explains precisely what information the App accesses, how it is used, where it is processed, and your rights regarding that information.

We have designed Wake Me with privacy as a foundation. Your location and alarm data are never transmitted to our servers, never sold, and never shared with advertisers. All core functionality runs entirely on your device.

🔒

Wake Me does not have user accounts, does not require registration, and does not collect any personally identifiable information. Your location data never leaves your device for the purpose of the app's alarm features.

Data Access

Information the App Accesses

Location Data

Wake Me requests access to your device's GPS location — both while the App is in the foreground and in the background. Location access is the core mechanism by which the App determines when you have entered the geofence (proximity zone) around your chosen destination.

  • Foreground location: Read continuously while the App is open and an alarm is active, to update the map and calculate distance to your destination in real time.
  • Background location: Read periodically (approximately every 3–5 seconds) by a system background task while an alarm is active and the App is minimised or the screen is locked. This is the only way the App can trigger an alarm when you are not actively using it.
  • Purpose limitation: Location coordinates are used solely to measure distance against your chosen alarm destination. They are never stored beyond the current session except as described under Local Storage below.

Device Identifiers & System State

The App reads device state such as whether location services are enabled and notification permission status, solely to prompt you for necessary permissions and display appropriate warnings.

Search Queries

When you type a destination in the search bar, your search text and — to obtain a map position — GPS coordinates are sent to OpenStreetMap's Nominatim geocoding service (see Third-Party Services below). We do not control OpenStreetMap's data handling.

Local Storage

Data Stored on Your Device

Wake Me stores data exclusively on your device using the standard React Native AsyncStorage API. This data is never transmitted to our servers. The following items are stored:

Active Alarm
The name, GPS coordinates, geofence radius, and chosen alarm sound of your current alarm. Stored while an alarm is active; deleted when the alarm is dismissed or cancelled.
Alarm State Flag
A boolean flag indicating whether the background task has detected arrival at your destination. Used to restore the alarm screen if the App was closed when the alarm triggered.
Saved Locations
An array of your favorited alarm locations (name, coordinates, radius, sound), saved automatically when you set an alarm. You can delete these at any time from the Saved Alarms screen.
History Log
A log of alarms you have previously dismissed, including the destination name, coordinates, and timestamp. Stored locally; you can clear this at any time.
Background Location Cache
The most recent GPS coordinate checked by the background task, cached briefly to improve performance. Cleared when tracking stops.
Notification State Flags
Temporary flags used to coordinate Stop/Snooze actions between the notification shade and the App UI, preventing duplicate actions. Cleared after use.
🗑️

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the App. Saved Locations and History can also be cleared individually within the App's "Saved Alarms" screen.

Third-Party Services

External Services We Use

Wake Me communicates with the following external services. No personal account data or device identifiers are sent to any of these services.

🗺️
OpenStreetMap Nominatim (Geocoding) When you search for a destination or tap a location on the map, your search text and/or approximate GPS coordinates are sent to Nominatim (nominatim.openstreetmap.org) to resolve a place name or street address. This service is operated by the OpenStreetMap Foundation. See their Privacy Policy.
🌍
OpenStreetMap Tile Server (Map Tiles) The interactive map inside the App loads map tile images from tile.openstreetmap.org. Tile requests include the geographic bounding box of the visible map area. No personally identifiable information is attached.
⚡
Expo OTA Updates (App Updates) On each App launch, a silent update check is performed against Expo's update delivery service (u.expo.dev). Only the App's current version identifier is exchanged. No location data or alarm data is involved. See Expo's Privacy Policy.
⚠️

Wake Me does not use any analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or behavioural tracking SDKs. No data is sent to A²H Software servers.

Data Use

How Information Is Used

  • Alarm triggering: Your real-time GPS coordinates are compared against your chosen destination using the Haversine formula to determine arrival. This computation happens entirely on your device.
  • Map display: Your location is rendered as a marker on the interactive map within the App.
  • Address resolution: Search queries and tap-coordinates are sent to Nominatim to resolve a human-readable location name. The result is displayed to you and, if you confirm, stored locally as part of your alarm configuration.
  • Notifications: When you arrive at a destination, a local notification is generated on your device. This notification is created by the App and delivered entirely by the device's operating system — no notification server is involved.
  • App updates: The Expo update service is queried on launch to silently deliver bug fixes and improvements.
Retention

Data Retention & Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of retention and deletion:

  • Active alarm data is automatically deleted when you cancel or dismiss an alarm.
  • Saved Locations can be individually deleted in the "Saved Alarms" screen, or cleared entirely by uninstalling the App.
  • History Log can be cleared at any time using the "Clear History Log" button in the "Saved Alarms" screen.
  • All App data is permanently deleted when you uninstall Wake Me from your device.

We do not retain any copies of your data on external servers because we do not receive it in the first place.

Children

Children's Privacy

Wake Me is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect information from children. Because the App does not collect or transmit personal data to us, there is no specific risk to children beyond general location permission use.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has granted location permissions to this App without your consent, you can revoke permissions at any time in your device's Settings.

Security

Security

Because Wake Me stores data only on your device using the operating system's standard application storage, your data is protected by your device's built-in security mechanisms (passcode, biometric lock, device encryption).

We do not operate servers that hold your data, so there is no server-side data breach risk for your location or alarm information.
