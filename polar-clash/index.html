1. Introduction

Thank you for choosing to play Polar Clash, a local 2-player pass-and-play magnet strategy board game. We highly value your trust and are fully committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data, showing how we treat your information.

Strict Privacy Commitment: Polar Clash is designed to operate 100% offline. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information of any kind.

2. Data Collection & Usage

Unlike many modern games, Polar Clash does not collect or require any personal data:

  • No Personal Information: We do not ask for, collect, or store personal details such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account details.
  • No Unique Device Identifiers: We do not track MAC addresses, IMEIs, Advertising IDs, or IP addresses.
  • No Location Data: We do not access or track your GPS location or IP-based geolocation.
  • No Gameplay Analytics: We do not monitor how long you play, what boards you select, or details of matches. All scores and game states are temporary and remain local to your game session.

3. Third-Party Services & Integrations

Polar Clash does not incorporate any third-party services that track or collect user information:

  • No Advertisements: The game does not serve ads, which means no ad networks can track your profile or behavior.
  • No Web Analytics: We do not embed services like Google Analytics, Firebase SDKs, or Flurry Analytics inside the mobile application.
  • No Social Media Integrations: There are no "Sign in with Facebook" or "Share on Twitter" integrations that could transmit your profile data.

4. Storage & Security

All data generated inside the game—such as your local sound volume preferences or whether you have accepted the Terms and Conditions—is stored locally on your device via standard local storage (AsyncStorage/Preferences).

Because the game operates entirely offline and lacks internet access permissions (network communication requests are disabled), there is no risk of remote data breaches, interception, or server-side leaks.

5. Children's Privacy

Polar Clash is a family-oriented strategy game. Because we do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information from any user, our application is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Family Policy requirements. 2. Description of the Game

Polar Clash is a local, 2-player pass-and-play turn-based strategy game. Players take turns placing octagonal 3D magnetic game pieces onto a spotlit digital tabletop board. When placed magnets clash, they are returned to the respective player's hand. The first player to empty their hand of magnets wins the match.
